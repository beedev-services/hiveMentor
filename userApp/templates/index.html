Welcome to Hive Mentor

Tech Enthusiasts helping Tech Enthusiasts with Mental Health

But our app isn't just about Mental Health, it's about networking and over all health too.

The what

A unique place to network, talk/chat, and keep logs.

We have 2 Distinct sides. The logs and the Chat. Chose where to start on login and registration

The Why

Why? Yes there are tons of applications out there that can help with mental health and logging things like your mood or food intake. But are they by geeks for geeks? Lets face it we speak a whole different language... literally at times. Because of this I wanted a place for us to help each other as sometimes only we know how.
